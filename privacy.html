Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

InvoiceFree.info ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password when you register
  • Invoice data: Business info, client details, and invoice content you create
  • Usage data: How you interact with our service (pages visited, features used)
  • Payment data: Processed securely by Stripe — we never store card numbers
  • Local data: Free plan data is stored in your browser's localStorage, not on our servers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our invoice generation service
  • To process payments (Pro plan) via Stripe
  • To send transactional emails (invoice confirmations, receipts)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our product

3. Data Storage and Security

Free plan users: All invoice data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not transmit or store this data on our servers.

Pro plan users: Invoice and account data is stored securely in encrypted databases. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Stripe: Payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy
  • EmailJS: Email delivery for invoice sending. Subject to EmailJS Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography. Subject to Google Privacy Policy

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for session management and analytics. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to third parties.

6. Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Export your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: privacy@invoicefree.info
