STEP 1 — DOWNLOAD YOUR ZIP FILE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Download the ZIP file Claude gave you
2. Find it in your Downloads folder
3. RIGHT-CLICK the ZIP file
4. Click "Extract All" (Windows) OR double-click (Mac)
5. You now have a folder called "invoicefree-v2"
6. Open that folder — you should see all the .html files

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 2 — SIGN UP FOR NETLIFY (FREE HOSTING)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Open browser → go to: https://www.netlify.com
2. Click "Sign up" button (top right corner)
3. Choose "Sign up with Email"
4. Enter your email and create a password
5. Check your email inbox
6. Click the confirmation link in the email
7. You are now inside Netlify!

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 3 — UPLOAD YOUR WEBSITE (DRAG & DROP!)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Inside Netlify, look for the section that says:
   "Want to deploy a new site without connecting to Git?"
   You will see a big box that says "Drag and drop"

2. Open your File Explorer (Windows) or Finder (Mac)
   Navigate to your "invoicefree-v2" folder

3. HOLD left mouse button on the folder
   DRAG it into the big Netlify box
   RELEASE mouse button

4. Wait 10-30 seconds while it uploads...

5. SUCCESS! Netlify gives you a random address like:
   https://wonderful-taffy-abc123.netlify.app

   COPY this address — this is your live website!

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
STEP 4 — CONNECT YOUR DOMAIN (invoicefree.info)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
You already own invoicefree.info. To use it with Netlify:

IN NETLIFY:
1. Click on your new site
2. Click "Domain settings" or "Set up a custom domain"
3. Type: invoicefree.info
4. Click "Verify" then "Add domain"
5. Netlify will show you NAMESERVER addresses like:
   dns1.p01.nsone.net
   dns2.p01.nsone.net
   (yours will be slightly different — note them down!)

WHERE YOU BOUGHT YOUR DOMAIN (GoDaddy / Namecheap / etc.):
1. Log in to your domain registrar
2. Find your domain: invoicefree.info
3. Find "DNS" or "Nameservers" settings
4. Change nameservers to the ones Netlify gave you
5. Save / Update

⏰ Wait 24-48 hours for the internet to update.
   After that, your website is live at invoicefree.info!

STEP 6 — UPDATING YOUR WEBSITE LATER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

IMPORTANT NOTES ABOUT DATA
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
⚠️  User accounts and invoice data is saved in the
    browser's localStorage. This means:
    
    - Each browser on each device has its own data
    - Clearing browser history will erase data
    - Data is NOT shared across devices
    
    This is perfect for getting started!
    
    For a REAL shared database (multiple devices,
    admin sees all users etc.) you need Firebase.
    
    Ask Claude: "Help me connect my InvoiceFree 
    website to Firebase Firestore database"
    
    Firebase has a free tier for small websites!
