How to Create a Professional Invoice in 5 Minutes (Free)

By InvoiceFree.info ·January 15, 2025 ·🕒 5 min read

Creating a professional invoice used to require expensive software or complicated spreadsheets. With a free invoice generator like InvoiceFree.info, you can create and send a stunning invoice in under 5 minutes — no account required.

Step 1: Fill in Your Business Details

Start with your name or company name, email, phone number, and address. This information appears at the top of every invoice. Use your real business name — it builds trust with clients and makes you look professional.

Step 2: Add Your Client's Information

Enter your client's company name, contact name, and email address. The email field is important — you'll use it to send the invoice directly from the app.

Step 3: Add Your Services as Line Items

Click "Add Item" and describe each service you provided. Add the quantity and rate, and the invoice automatically calculates totals, taxes, and discounts. No math needed.

Pro Tip: Use specific descriptions like "Website Homepage Design — 8 hours @ $75/hr" instead of just "Design work". Clear descriptions reduce client disputes and speed up payment.

Step 4: Choose a Professional Template

InvoiceFree.info offers 5 professional templates — from Classic Professional (clean and minimal, free) to Elegant Dark (premium gold design, Pro). Select the one that matches your brand.

Step 5: Download PDF or Share a Link

Click the PDF button to download a print-ready invoice, or use the Share Link button to send your client a live URL they can view on any device. Both options are available on the free plan.
